RDU Workflow

Standard operating process for every job — direct client and builder-referred.

Direct Client Jobs

01
Initial Enquiry

Client contacts via website, Rocky chatbot, or referral. Log as lead immediately in the dashboard with source tagged.

02
Site Inspection

Book within 48hrs. Harry or Ronny attends. Record all findings in the Inspections module — photos, measurements, issues noted.

03
Quote Preparation

Itemised quote issued within 24hrs of inspection. Include cut list if Colorbond. Payment terms: 10% deposit, 40% materials, 30% progress, 20% retention.

04
Quote Acceptance

Client signs off. Deposit invoice generated automatically. Job added to schedule. Crew briefed. Lead status updated to Converted.

05
Works on Site

Harry leads crew. Daily Activity Reports filed. Progress photos at each milestone. Job status updated in dashboard.

06
Completion and Sign-Off

Walkthrough with client before final invoice. Site left clean. Job status set to Completed. Request Google review.

Builder-Referred Jobs

01
Builder Referral

Builder contacts Ronny directly. Log in leads with source tagged as "Builder Referral" and builder name in notes.

02
Plans and Scope Review

Obtain plans from builder. Review scope. Confirm inclusions and exclusions in writing before quoting.

03
Quote to Builder

Quote issued to builder, not end client. Separate pricing from direct client rates. Include provisional sums if plans incomplete.

04
Scheduling

Coordinate with builder's program. Confirm slab/frame readiness before committing crew. Add to schedule with builder contact noted.

05
Works and Variations

All variations approved in writing before proceeding. Harry to notify Ronny of any scope changes same day they arise.

06
Practical Completion

Invoice builder on PC. Note defects liability period. Follow up for referrals to next project.
